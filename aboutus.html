About us

We are a group of young enthusiasts residing in Amman, who came together to start this car project. It is not just a car finding website but a website for car lovers to connect and share their cars with each other.At Morscars, we make selling your car a hassle-free experience. Whether you're upgrading to a new vehicle or simply looking to sell, we're here to assist you every step of the way..

Why Sell With Us?

  • Convenience: We offer a convenient and straightforward selling process, saving you time and effort.
  • Fair Market Value: Our team evaluates your vehicle to provide you with a fair market value, ensuring you get the best price possible.
  • Wide Reach: Utilizing our extensive network, we connect you with potential buyers, expanding your selling opportunities.
  • Professional Assistance: Our experienced staff provides professional assistance and guidance throughout the selling process, addressing any questions or concerns you may have.
  • Secure Transactions: We prioritize the security of your transactions, ensuring a safe and reliable selling experience.

How It Works

  1. Submit Your Vehicle Information: Provide details about your vehicle using our online form or contact us directly.
  2. Evaluation: Our team evaluates your vehicle's condition, mileage, and other factors to determine its market value.
  3. Agree on Price: Once the evaluation is complete, we discuss the proposed price with you, ensuring transparency and fairness.
  4. Finalize Transaction: Upon agreement, we finalize the transaction, handling all necessary paperwork and logistics.
  5. Receive Payment: You receive payment promptly, completing the selling process with ease.
